What is Panily?


Healthcare is complex and managing your healthcare - keeping track of visits, reports, medications, symptoms, notes from each visit etc. - can feel like a full time job.

We feel your pain and that's why we're building Panily:

  • Panily was built around your needs as a patient
  • You can manage and track your healthcare events and interactions using Panily
  • You control your data and access to it
  • And we are working on much, much more in 2023

Why did we build Panily?


Healthcare is complex and managing your healthcare - keeping track of visits, reports, medications, symptoms, notes from each visit etc. - can feel like a full time job. Panily was built so patients and caregivers dealing with complex healthcare needs have digital tools to help manage their healthcare all in one app:

  • Record your conditions and symptoms
  • Get reminders about your medications and for refills
  • Keep track of healthcare visits and their outcomes
  • Save all your reports, etc in one place
