Track Medications

Track prescriptions and medications in the prescription from one place. Set up reminders to take your medications, log any medications you've taken and get refill reminders, all from within the app.

Care Team

Record and access all your care team contacts from one place. Save your physicians, care locations and pharmacies in one easy to use place, call your care team or get directions to their locations from your address book in the app.

Save Reports

Save your lab reports and other medical files securely on your phone within the app. You can load the files from your device or take a picture to save them. Access them anytime anywhere from your phone.

Add Conditions

Add your conditions either from the Panily condition library or manually, add symptoms, and any factors that may make your condition better or worse, so you can start tracking them using Panily

Record Symptoms

Record any symptoms you want to track. View your symptoms in fine detail over time, so you can spot any trends that might help you manage your condition.

Journal your day

Keep a daily journal from within the Panily app, so you can also track other factors that might affect your conditions or mental state. Add helpful tags to record events during the day and how you're feeling.

Track Appointments

Save medical events and appointments. Get reminders for your appointments, link to any reports/handouts from your appointment from within the app.

Get Reminders

Setup and get reminders for specific medical or healthcare events. Coming in 2023 - advanced reminders to better deal with the complexity of managing your health.

Insurance cards

Take images of your insurance cards and organize them from within the app, so you never have to worry about forgetting them at home.
